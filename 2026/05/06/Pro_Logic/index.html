Discrete Mathematics 1 : Propositional Logic

Propositional Logic(SJTU CS2501H)

Definition 1:

  • ASSUMPTION:

If φ∈Φ,then Φ⊢​φIf \ \varphi \in \Phi,then\ \Phi \vdash​ \varphi If φ∈Φ,then Φ⊢​φ

  • WEAKENING:

If Φ⊆Φ′ and Φ⊢ψ,then Φ′⊢ψIf \ \Phi \subseteq \Phi' \ and \ \Phi \vdash \psi, then \ \Phi' \vdash \psi If Φ⊆Φ′ and Φ⊢ψ,then Φ′⊢ψ

  • SUBSTITUTION:

If Φ⊢ψ and Φ,ψ⊢χ,then Φ⊢χIf \ \Phi \vdash \psi \ and \ \Phi , \psi \vdash \chi, then \ \Phi \vdash \chi If Φ⊢ψ and Φ,ψ⊢χ,then Φ⊢χ

Example 1. Prove the following derivations using ASSUMPTION, WEAKENING and SUBSTITUTION.

ϕ⊢ϕ\phi \vdash \phiϕ⊢ϕ .

If ϕ⊢ψ\phi \vdash \psiϕ⊢ψ and ψ⊢χ\psi \vdash \chiψ⊢χ , then ϕ⊢χ\phi \vdash \chiϕ⊢χ .

If Φ,ϕ⊢ψ\Phi ,\phi \vdash \psiΦ,ϕ⊢ψ and Φ,ψ⊢χ\Phi ,\psi \vdash \chiΦ,ψ⊢χ , then Φ,ϕ⊢χ\Phi ,\phi \vdash \chiΦ,ϕ⊢χ .

Proof.

(1) ϕ⊢ϕ\phi \vdash \phiϕ⊢ϕ (by ASSUMPTION).

(1) ϕ⊢ψ\phi \vdash \psiϕ⊢ψ (hypothesis).

(2) ψ⊢χ\psi \vdash \chiψ⊢χ (hypothesis).

(3) ϕ,ψ⊢χ\phi ,\psi \vdash \chiϕ,ψ⊢χ (WEAKENING and (2)).

(4) ϕ⊢χ\phi \vdash \chiϕ⊢χ (SUBSTITUTION and (1) and (3)).

(1) Φ,ϕ⊢ψ\Phi ,\phi \vdash \psiΦ,ϕ⊢ψ (hypothesis).

(2) Φ,ψ⊢χ\Phi ,\psi \vdash \chiΦ,ψ⊢χ (hypothesis).

(3) Φ,ϕ,ψ⊢χ\Phi ,\phi ,\psi \vdash \chiΦ,ϕ,ψ⊢χ (WEAKENING and (2)).

(4) Φ,ϕ⊢χ\Phi ,\phi \vdash \chiΦ,ϕ⊢χ (SUBSTITUTION and (1) and (3)).

Definition 2:

  • AND-INTRO:

φ,ψ⊢φ∧ψ\varphi, \psi \vdash \varphi \land \psi φ,ψ⊢φ∧ψ

  • AND-ELIM1:

φ∧ψ⊢φ\varphi \land \psi \vdash \varphi φ∧ψ⊢φ

  • AND-ELIM2:

φ∧ψ⊢ψ\varphi \land \psi \vdash \psi φ∧ψ⊢ψ

Example 2. Prove the following derivations.

If Φ⊢ψ\Phi \vdash \psiΦ⊢ψ and Φ⊢χ\Phi \vdash \chiΦ⊢χ , then Φ⊢ψ∧χ\Phi \vdash \psi \wedge \chiΦ⊢ψ∧χ .

If Φ⊢ψ∧χ\Phi \vdash \psi \wedge \chiΦ⊢ψ∧χ , then Φ⊢ψ\Phi \vdash \psiΦ⊢ψ .

If Φ⊢ψ∧χ\Phi \vdash \psi \wedge \chiΦ⊢ψ∧χ , then Φ⊢χ\Phi \vdash \chiΦ⊢χ .

If Φ,ψ1∧ψ2⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ1​∧ψ2​⊢χ ,then Φ,ψ1,ψ2⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{1},\psi_{2}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ1​,ψ2​⊢χ .

If Φ,ψ1⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{1}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ1​⊢χ,then Φ,ψ1∧ψ2⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ1​∧ψ2​⊢χ .

If Φ,ψ2⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{2}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ2​⊢χ,then Φ,ψ1∧ψ2⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ1​∧ψ2​⊢χ

Proof.

(1) ψ,χ⊢ψ∧χ\psi ,\chi \vdash \psi \wedge \chiψ,χ⊢ψ∧χ (AND-INTRO).

(2) Φ,ψ,χ⊢ψ∧χ\Phi ,\psi ,\chi \vdash \psi \wedge \chiΦ,ψ,χ⊢ψ∧χ (WEAKENING and (1)).

(3) Φ⊢χ\Phi \vdash \chiΦ⊢χ (hypothesis).

(4) Φ,ψ⊢χ\Phi ,\psi \vdash \chiΦ,ψ⊢χ (WEAKENING and (3)).

(5) Φ,ψ⊢ψ∧χ\Phi ,\psi \vdash \psi \wedge \chiΦ,ψ⊢ψ∧χ (SUBSTITUTION and (2) and (4)).

(6) Φ⊢ψ\Phi \vdash \psiΦ⊢ψ (hypothesis).

(7) Φ⊢ψ∧χ\Phi \vdash \psi \wedge \chiΦ⊢ψ∧χ (SUBSTITUTION and (5) and (7)).

(1) Φ⊢ψ∧χ\Phi \vdash \psi \wedge \chiΦ⊢ψ∧χ (hypothesis).

(2) ψ∧χ⊢ψ\psi \wedge \chi \vdash \psiψ∧χ⊢ψ (AND-ELIM1).

(3) Φ,ψ∧χ⊢ψ\Phi ,\psi \wedge \chi \vdash \psiΦ,ψ∧χ⊢ψ (WEAKENING and (2)).

(4) Φ⊢ψ\Phi \vdash \psiΦ⊢ψ (SUBSTITUTION and (1) and (3)).

Similar.

(1) ψ1,ψ2⊢ψ1∧ψ2\psi_{1},\psi_{2}\vdash \psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2}ψ1​,ψ2​⊢ψ1​∧ψ2​ (AND-INTRO).

(2) Φ,ψ1,ψ2⊢ψ1∧ψ2\Phi ,\psi_{1},\psi_{2}\vdash \psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2}Φ,ψ1​,ψ2​⊢ψ1​∧ψ2​ (WEAKENING and (1)).

(3) Φ,ψ1∧ψ2⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ1​∧ψ2​⊢χ (hypothesis).

(4) Φ,ψ1,ψ2,ψ1∧ψ2⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{1},\psi_{2},\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ1​,ψ2​,ψ1​∧ψ2​⊢χ (WEAKENING and (3)).

(5) Φ,ψ1∧ψ2⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ1​∧ψ2​⊢χ (SUBSTITUTION and (2) and (4)).

Definition 3:

We write φ⊣⊢ψ\varphi \dashv\vdash \psiφ⊣⊢ψ, if and only if both φ⊢ψ\varphi \vdash \psiφ⊢ψ and ψ⊢φ\psi \vdash \varphiψ⊢φ.

Theorem 1:

  • AND-L:

 If Φ,ψ1,ψ2⊢χ,then  Φ,ψ1∧ψ2⊢χ\ If \ \Phi , \psi_1, \psi_2 \vdash \chi, then \ \ \Phi , \psi_1 \land \psi_2 \vdash \chi  If Φ,ψ1​,ψ2​⊢χ,then  Φ,ψ1​∧ψ2​⊢χ

  • AND-COMM:

φ∧ψ⊣⊢ψ∧φ\varphi \land \psi \dashv\vdash \psi \land \varphi φ∧ψ⊣⊢ψ∧φ

  • AND-ASSOC:

(φ∧ψ)∧χ⊣⊢φ∧(ψ∧χ)(\varphi \land \psi) \land \chi \dashv\vdash \varphi \land (\psi \land \chi) (φ∧ψ)∧χ⊣⊢φ∧(ψ∧χ)

Proof of AND- L:

(1) Φ,ψ1,ψ2⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{1},\psi_{2}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ1​,ψ2​⊢χ (hypothesis).

(2) ψ1∧ψ2⊢ψ1\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2}\vdash \psi_{1}ψ1​∧ψ2​⊢ψ1​ (AND- ELIM1).

(3) Φ,ψ1∧ψ2⊢ψ1\Phi ,\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2}\vdash \psi_{1}Φ,ψ1​∧ψ2​⊢ψ1​ (WEAKENING and (2)).

(4) ψ1∧ψ2⊢ψ2\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2}\vdash \psi_{2}ψ1​∧ψ2​⊢ψ2​ (AND- ELIM2).

(5) Φ,ψ1∧ψ2,ψ1⊢ψ2\Phi ,\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2},\psi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2}Φ,ψ1​∧ψ2​,ψ1​⊢ψ2​ (WEAKENING and (4)).

(6) Φ,ψ1∧ψ2,ψ1,ψ2⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2},\psi_{1},\psi_{2}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ1​∧ψ2​,ψ1​,ψ2​⊢χ (WEAKENING and (1)).

(7) Φ,ψ1∧ψ2,ψ1⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2},\psi_{1}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ1​∧ψ2​,ψ1​⊢χ (SUBSTITUTION and (5) and (6)).

(8) Φ,ψ1∧ψ2⊢χ\Phi ,\psi_{1}\wedge \psi_{2}\vdash \chiΦ,ψ1​∧ψ2​⊢χ (SUBSTITUTION and (3) and (7)).

Proof of AND- COMM:

It suffices to prove: φ∧ψ⊢ψ∧φ\varphi \wedge \psi \vdash \psi \wedge \varphiφ∧ψ⊢ψ∧φ

(1) φ∧ψ⊢ψ\varphi \wedge \psi \vdash \psiφ∧ψ⊢ψ (AND- ELIM2).

(2) φ∧ψ⊢φ\varphi \wedge \psi \vdash \varphiφ∧ψ⊢φ (AND- ELIM1).

(3) φ∧ψ⊢ψ∧φ\varphi \wedge \psi \vdash \psi \wedge \varphiφ∧ψ⊢ψ∧φ (AND- R and (1) and (2)).

Proof of AND- ASSOC:

From left to right:

(1) φ,ψ,χ⊢φ\varphi ,\psi ,\chi \vdash \varphiφ,ψ,χ⊢φ (ASSUMPTION).

(2) (φ∧ψ),χ⊢φ(\varphi \wedge \psi),\chi \vdash \varphi(φ∧ψ),χ⊢φ (AND- L and (1)).

(3) (φ∧ψ)∧χ⊢φ(\varphi \wedge \psi)\wedge \chi \vdash \varphi(φ∧ψ)∧χ⊢φ (AND- L and (2)).

(4) φ,ψ,χ⊢φ\varphi ,\psi ,\chi \vdash \varphiφ,ψ,χ⊢φ (ASSUMPTION).

(5) (φ∧ψ),χ⊢φ\left(\varphi \wedge \psi\right),\chi \vdash \varphi(φ∧ψ),χ⊢φ (AND- L and (4)).

(6) (φ∧ψ)∧χ⊢ψ(\varphi \wedge \psi)\wedge \chi \vdash \psi(φ∧ψ)∧χ⊢ψ (AND- L and (5)).

(7) (φ∧ψ)∧χ⊢χ(\varphi \wedge \psi)\wedge \chi \vdash \chi(φ∧ψ)∧χ⊢χ (AND- ELIM2).

(8) (φ∧ψ)∧χ⊢(ψ∧χ)(\varphi \wedge \psi)\wedge \chi \vdash (\psi \wedge \chi)(φ∧ψ)∧χ⊢(ψ∧χ) (AND- R and (6) and (7)).

(9) (φ∧ψ)∧χ⊢φ∧(ψ∧χ)(\varphi \wedge \psi)\wedge \chi \vdash \varphi \wedge (\psi \wedge \chi)(φ∧ψ)∧χ⊢φ∧(ψ∧χ) (AND- R and (3) and (8)).

Example 3. Prove the following derivations.

If ψ1⊢ψ2\psi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2}ψ1​⊢ψ2​ and χ1⊢χ2\chi_{1}\vdash \chi_{2}χ1​⊢χ2​ , then ψ1∧χ1⊢ψ2∧χ2\psi_{1}\wedge \chi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2}\wedge \chi_{2}ψ1​∧χ1​⊢ψ2​∧χ2​ .

Proof.

(1) ψ1⊢ψ2\psi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2}ψ1​⊢ψ2​ (hypothesis).

(2) ψ1,χ1⊢ψ2\psi_{1},\chi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2}ψ1​,χ1​⊢ψ2​ (WEAKENING and (1)).

(3) χ1⊢χ2\chi_{1}\vdash \chi_{2}χ1​⊢χ2​ (hypothesis).

(4) ψ1,χ1⊢χ2\psi_{1},\chi_{1}\vdash \chi_{2}ψ1​,χ1​⊢χ2​ (WEAKENING and (3)).

(5) ψ1,χ1⊢ψ2∧χ2\psi_{1},\chi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2}\wedge \chi_{2}ψ1​,χ1​⊢ψ2​∧χ2​ (AND- R and (2) and (4)).

(6) ψ1∧χ1⊢ψ2∧χ2\psi_{1}\wedge \chi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2}\wedge \chi_{2}ψ1​∧χ1​⊢ψ2​∧χ2​ (AND- L and (5)).

Theorem 2:

If Φ,ψ1⊢ψ2 and Φ,χ1⊢χ2, then Φ,ψ1∧χ1⊢ψ2∧χ2If \ \Phi ,\psi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2} \ and \ \Phi ,\chi_{1}\vdash \chi_{2} , \ then \ \Phi ,\psi_{1}\wedge \chi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2}\wedge \chi_{2} If Φ,ψ1​⊢ψ2​ and Φ,χ1​⊢χ2​, then Φ,ψ1​∧χ1​⊢ψ2​∧χ2​

Proof.

(1) Φ,ψ1⊢ψ2\Phi ,\psi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2}Φ,ψ1​⊢ψ2​ (hypothesis).

(2) Φ,ψ1,χ1⊢ψ2\Phi ,\psi_{1},\chi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2}Φ,ψ1​,χ1​⊢ψ2​ (WEAKENING and (1)).

(3) Φ,χ1⊢χ2\Phi ,\chi_{1}\vdash \chi_{2}Φ,χ1​⊢χ2​ (hypothesis).

(4) Φ,ψ1,χ1⊢χ2\Phi ,\psi_{1},\chi_{1}\vdash \chi_{2}Φ,ψ1​,χ1​⊢χ2​ (WEAKENING and (3)).

(5) Φ,ψ1,χ1⊢ψ2∧χ2\Phi ,\psi_{1},\chi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2}\wedge \chi_{2}Φ,ψ1​,χ1​⊢ψ2​∧χ2​ (AND- R and (2) and (4)).

(6) Φ,ψ1∧χ1⊢ψ2∧χ2\Phi ,\psi_{1}\wedge \chi_{1}\vdash \psi_{2}\wedge \chi_{2}Φ,ψ1​∧χ1​⊢ψ2​∧χ2​ (AND- L and (5)).

Definition 4:

  • OR- INTRO1: $$\varphi \vdash \varphi \vee \psi$$

  • OR- INTRO2: $$\psi \vdash \varphi \vee \psi$$

  • OR- ELIM (OR- L): $$If \ \Phi ,\psi_{1}\vdash \chi \ and \ \Phi ,\ \psi_{2}\vdash \chi \ , then \ \Phi ,\psi_{1}\vee \psi_{2}\vdash \chi$$
